Nobody Fits in a Box!

Judgement-free healthcare

We take the time to get to know you and provide care that is right for your unique self.

Canopy Medical Clinic is a Women’s and Gender Healthcare clinic located in Fargo, North Dakota. Throughout every stage of life, people have unique healthcare needs. LGBTQ indiviuals especially face discrimination and misunderstanding from healthcare providers. We strive to treat all with respect and dignity. That’s why we won’t rush you out the door. We take the time to get to know you, so that we can provide an individual, evidence-based care plan that works best for you. When individual people are healthy, families and communities are healthy, too.

Heidi Selzler-Echola

Heidi is a board certified Women's and Gender Health Nurse Practitioner licensed in both North Dakota and Minnesota. She graduated from North Dakota State University with Bachelor's in Nursing and Anthropology, with a minor in Women's and Gender Studies. She attended University of Cincinnati to obtain her Master's in Advanced Practice Registered Nursing.

Heidi has spent most of her nursing career working in women's reproductive health, but has also worked in family practice, long-term care, a transgender hormone clinic and in-patient psychiatric care. Her passions involve reproductive justice, public health and addressing gaps in access to care.

Payment & Insurance

We accept most major insurance plans. It is always recommended to call your insurance company for details.

Self-pay policy:
Initial visit/annual exams: $200-$300 based on health concerns, length, history, labs, etc.

All self-pay patients must pay $100 at an intial exam and $50 at recheck paid upfront before the visit. Canopy will work with you to pay your bill. Payment plans are an option. Contact us for more information.
