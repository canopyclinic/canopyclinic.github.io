Heidi Selzler-Echola

Heidi is a board certified Women's and Gender Health Nurse Practitioner licensed in both North Dakota and Minnesota. She graduated from North Dakota State University with Bachelor's in Nursing and Anthropology, with a minor in Women's and Gender Studies. She attended The University of Cincinnati to obtain her Master's in Advanced Practice Registered Nursing.

Heidi has spent most of her nursing career working in women's reproductive health, but has also worked in family practice, long-term care, a transgender hormone clinic and in-patient psychiatric care. Her passions involve reproductive justice, public health and addressing gaps in access to care.
